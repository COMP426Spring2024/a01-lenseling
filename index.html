The Monkey King

孙悟空 Sūn Wù Kōng

Background

The Monkey King (Sun Wukong 孫悟空) is an important figure in Chinese Mythology who is a main character in the adventure novel Journey to the West written by Wu Cheng'en. He is blessed with superstrength and the ability to transform shape into different animals and objects. He tends to be impatient, and behaves like a trickster.

Names and Titles

  • Shí Hóu (石猴) Stone monkey
  • Měi Hóuwáng (美猴王) Handsome Monkey-King
  • Sūn Wùkōng (孫悟空) Monkey-King
  • Bìmǎwēn (弼馬溫) Keeper of the Heavenly Horses
  • Qítiān Dàshèng (齊天大聖) The Great Sage, Heaven's Equal
  • Xíngzhě (行者) Wandering Monk
